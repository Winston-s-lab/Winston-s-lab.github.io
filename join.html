Join us!

The lab invites and welcomes highly motivated and passionate individuals (local and overseas) to join the team. The lab is now recruiting for multiple junior and senior positions. The successful candidate will join a vibrant scientific team engaging in both basic science and translational research. The lab provides a nurturing environment that encourages creative thinking and innovative approaches to tackle the most challenging scientific questions in the stem cell field. Candidate will also have opportunities to work with partnering scientist and clinicians from other institutes to conduct interdisciplinary research.

Positions available:

Post-doctoral Fellow / 博士后

  1. A Ph.D. degree in life sciences or relevant field of study.
  2. Candidates proficient in primary cell culture and cell imaging techniques preferred. 
  3. Candidates proficient in single cell profiling techniques preferred.
  4. Candidates with experience and publications in stem cell studies will be strongly considered. 

Associate Professor/ 副研究员

  1. A Ph.D. degree in life sciences or relevant field of study.
  2. Postdoctoral training in relevant field of study with good publication track record. Recent Ph.D. graduate with excellent research track record will also be considered.
  3. Proficient in English (speech and writing). Candidates with grant writing and management experience are preferred.
  4. Candidates will have the opportunity to develop projects and lead a small research team.

Research Assistant positions / 研究实习员

  1. At least a Master’s degree in life sciences or relevant field of study.
  2. Candidates with experience in culturing primary human cells and confocal imaging will be strongly considered.
  3. Candidates with experience in mouse work will be strongly considered.
