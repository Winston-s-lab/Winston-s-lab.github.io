Join us!

The lab invites and welcomes highly motivated and committed scientists (local and overseas) to join the team. The lab is now recruiting both fresh graduates and experience postdoc for junior and senior positions to build a world class lab in stem cell research. The successful candidate will join a vibrant scientific environment devoted to basic science and translational research. The lab provides a nurturing environment that encourages creative thinking and innovative approaches to tackle the most challenging scientific questions in the stem cell field. Candidate will also have opportunities to work with scientist of partner institutes to conduct interdisciplinary research to address high-priority projects in regenerative medicine.

Positions available:

Post-doctoral Fellow / 博士后

  1. A Ph.D. degree in biology or relevant field of study.
  2. Proficient in primary cell culture and cell imaging techniques.
  3. Experience in handling primary cell culture and genetic manipulation techniques will be a plus.
  4. Candidates with publications in relevant stem cell studies will be strongly considered. 

Associate Professor/ 副研究员

  1. A Ph.D. degree in life sciences or relevant field of study.
  2. Postdoctoral training in relevant field of study with good publication track record. Recent Ph.D. graduate with excellent research track record will also be considered.
  3. Candidates who have extensive experienced in handling primary cell culture or with publications in stem cell research are preferred.
  4. Candidates experienced with pre-clinical models are preferred.
  5. Proficient in English (listening, speaking, writing and reading). Candidates with grant writing and management experience are preferred.

Research Assistant positions / 科研助理

  1. At least a Master’s degree in biology or relevant field of study
  2. Candidates with work experience as research assistant will.
  3. Experience in cell culture and cell imaging techniques.
  4. Experience in other molecular and cellular research techniques is a plus.
  5. Candidates with experience in mouse work will be strongly considered.
