Liver regeneration

Liver regeneration from acute injury is always beneficial and has been extensively studied. Experimental models that involve partial hepatectomy or chemical injury have revealed extracellular and intracellular signalling pathways that are used to return the liver to equivalent size and weight to those prior to injury.

Molecular cloning

Molecular cloning is an essential technique to create DNA-based experimental tools for expression in bacterial or mammalian cells. Examples of such DNA constructs include a promoter element fused to a reporter gene or a cDNA sequence under the control of a ubiquitous promoter.
